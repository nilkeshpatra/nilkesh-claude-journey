The problem today

Customer payment data is scattered across multiple disconnected systems. When something goes wrong, we find out late — and fixing it takes hours of manual investigation across siloed tools. Customers experience the impact before we do.

What the Observatory is

A unified, real-time monitoring platform that aggregates all customer-facing transaction signals into one place. It sits on top of existing infrastructure — no new payment systems — and gives every team a shared, live view of payment health.
